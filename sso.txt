1. Goto Active Directory (Default)
2. Enterprise Applications > Click "New Application"
3. Select "Box" Click "Add" Button
4. From List of Applications click on "Box"
5. Goto "Users & Groups" click "New User"
	choose your guest user from the list and ADD it
	Assign Role "User"
	Click Apply
6. Click on "Single Sign On" and choose "Password"
7. Open new private browser windows
	and Visit https://myapps.microsoft.com
8. Login with Guest User credentials
    You should get "Box" application logo
